# Your CMS Dashboard

## Welcome to the CMS

This website uses Strapi as a content management system (CMS) where you can update website content without touching any code. Think of it as the control center for managing text, images, blog posts, and more.

## Getting Around

When you log in, you'll see a menu on the left. Here's what you'll use most:

### Home

Your starting point after logging in. Shows recent activity and quick links.

### Content Manager

**This is where you'll spend most of your time.** Here you can:
- Edit pages and blog posts
- Update navigation and footer (under the "Global" entry)
- Manage all website content

### Media Library

Where all images and files are stored. Upload new images here before adding them to pages.
